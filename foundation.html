The Foundation

At Perry Meridian High School, you are definitely welcomed into new territory when you walk into one of the school's bathrooms. At first glance may seem like a normal bathroom, but when you take just one extra second to look closer, you are then slapped with horror.

Some examples of these:

  • Stall Doors with Massive Gaps
  • Toilet/Sewage Water Flooding the Floor
  • Clogged Sinks
  • No Toilet Paper
  • No Paper Towels
  • No Soap
  • No Normal Smell
  • No Locks

Overall, it is not the best.
